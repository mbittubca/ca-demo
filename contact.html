FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive chartered accountancy services including tax planning & filing, audit & assurance, GST compliance, company registration, bookkeeping, payroll services, and business advisory.

Q: Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer a free initial consultation to understand your requirements and explain how we can help. This helps us provide you with an accurate quote for our services.

Q: What are your fees?

Our fees vary based on the service and complexity of work. We believe in transparent pricing with no hidden charges. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

Q: Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with startups and offer special packages for new businesses. From company registration to ongoing compliance, we'll be your financial partner from day one.
