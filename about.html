Our Story

Building Trust Since 1999

Sharma & Associates was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide exceptional financial services that help businesses grow while maintaining the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a small practice to one of the most trusted chartered accountancy firms in the region. Our journey has been marked by a commitment to excellence, continuous learning, and building lasting relationships with our clients.

Today, we serve over 500 clients across diverse industries, from ambitious startups to established corporations. Our team of 15+ qualified professionals brings together expertise in taxation, audit, compliance, and business advisory.
